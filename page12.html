Secret Tools

There are 2 secret tools I want you to learn about money management:

Delayed Gratification: Imagine you have a big bowl of your favorite candy, like yummy gummy bears. Delayed gratification means instead of gobbling them up right away, you save some for later.

Thoughtful Spending Decisions: Every time you get money, you have a choice to make: how to use it wisely. Making thoughtful spending decisions means thinking carefully about what you want and need - saving your money for something really important or like buying a toy you've been wanting

>Remember: Both delayed gratification and making thoughtful spending decisions are like magical tools. They help you make the most out of your money, so you can enjoy awesome things now and in the future!
