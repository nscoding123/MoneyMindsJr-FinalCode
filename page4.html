Saving Money

When you take a portion of your earnings, and instead of spending it now, you put it away to use later to achieve a goal, it is called 'Saving'. Saving goals can be short-term or long-term.

Saving money for things you want to buy soon, like in a few weeks or months, is called saving for a short-term goal.

Saving money for things that are far away, like a few years from now, is called saving for a long-term goal.

Remember: Saving is like a superpower that helps you make your wishes and dreams come true!
